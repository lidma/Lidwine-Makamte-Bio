About Me

Lidwine Makamte

lidwine Makamte is a freelance graphic designer and brand strategist with a passion for creating elegant, minimal, and clean designs that help businesses succeed. With a keen eye for detail and a love for natural aesthetics, I aim to bring out the best in every project I work on. I also have a background in healthcare and business. As someone who loves to help people, I find great satisfaction in seeing my work help others achieve their goals

As a skilled brand expert, I have a diverse background that includes a high professional diploma in physiotherapy from St. Louis University of Health and Biomedical Science in Cameroon, a Master's in Business Administration from Nanjing University of Post and Telecommunication in China, and extensive training in digital marketing, front-end development, and graphic design.

My experience working in various hospitals and becoming a member of the Cameroon Red Cross helped me to hone my communication and problem-solving skills, while studying Chinese at Guangxi College of Science and Economic in China taught me the importance of cultural awareness and effective communication. These skills have been invaluable in my career as a brand expert, where I help businesses reach their target audience and achieve their goals. To further enhance my skill set, I took online courses on digital marketing, front-end development, and graphic design. These courses have allowed me to apply my creativity and technical expertise to help businesses develop effective branding strategies and create impactful marketing campaigns.

As Winston Churchill once said, "Success is not final, failure is not fatal: it is the courage to continue that counts." This quote resonates with me because it emphasizes the importance of perseverance in the face of challenges. I approach every project with this mindset, and it has allowed me to excel as a brand expert, providing valuable insights to businesses.As a driven and detail-oriented individual with a passion for helping businesses succeed, I am confident that my diverse background and skills make me a valuable asset to any team.
